REGISTRATION & ADMISSION

The registration for the new admissions commences in the month of January every year. The new admission is open for Nursery and for a limited number of seats vacant in other classes. The children who have completed three (Plus, Minus three months as on 1st March) years of age are eligible for registration to Nursery. The admission to other classes is done if a vacancy exists. The request for registration is a prerequisite and doesn’t ensure admission. The registration charges are non-refundable. The decision of the Principal in all matters concerning admission shall be final and binding. The admission may be refused to any candidate without assigning any reason.

Registration and Admission for Toddler’s & Primary Wing commences in the month of December every year. The admission is done as per the given age of the child:

  • Nursery 3+
  • LKG 4+
  • UKG 5+
  • The admission to other classes is done if a vacancy exists.
  • The registration charges are non-refundable. The school will not be responsible if a child, who is registered, but fails to appear in the entrance test.
  • The registration form must be complete in all respects at the time of submission to the office.

DOCUMENTS REQUIRED AT THE TIME OF ADMISSION

The registration form must be submitted along with a Photocopy of the under mentioned documents:

  • Birth Certificate, issued by a valid authority like Municipal Committee / CMO.
  • Aadhar Card (Ward and Parents)
  • Transfer Certificate in original.
  • Migration Certificate in original (class XI & XII),
  • Report Card of the Last class attended / D.M.C.
  • Two latest coloured passport size photographs of the child.
  • Immunization Card.

*The registration is a prerequisite and does not ensure admission.

WITHDRAWALS

In case a child has to be withdrawn from the school, one month prior notice is required to be given in writing by the parents to the Principal. The School Leaving Certificate is issued on a payment of Rs. 50/- and after the clearance of the arrears and other dues.

  • Parents are requested to give one month notice failing which fee for one month will be charged.
  • Transfer Certificates are not issued until all dues of the school are settled.
  • It will take 7 working days after clearance to process the application for withdrawal, so ensure timely approval for the same.
  • The school is authorized to give only the TC, any other counter singing by relevant authorities, (State/ District) is the responsibility of the parent.

SUSPENSION AND RUSTICATION

A child found guilty of creating any sort of nuisance in the class/school/damaging the school property/using abusive or vulgar language against the fellow students/school mates/teachers/members of non-teaching staff or hospitality staff, will be given two warning letters in the first instance and subsequently he/she will be suspended for a week. The student will be rusticated in case of repetition of above said behavioral problems.
