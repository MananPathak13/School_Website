Our Journey to Excellence

The S. D. Public Senior Secondary is an elixir of knowledge and aims at providing the possible best in academics, sports, co-curricular activities so as to make its students multifaceted personalities and responsible citizens. The school owes a lot to its founder Late Dr. Sanatan Kumar Pathak ji who believed that the educational institutes are temples of learning. The foundation of the school was laid in 2004 by the S. D. Educational & Social Welfare Society (Regd.), Amloh Road, Khanna (Pb.) and over the years, the institution has grown in strength and size. Now a lot many students are getting quality education in the sprawling campus. The cumulative experience of all these years has molded the school into a multi-disciplinary and multi-dimensional sanctum of learning and knowledge; where the students are shaped with love and care to elicit their talent, potential as well as to induce new skills to prepare them to face the world.

Mission and Vision

We have a broad mission to nurture a feeling of love and care through our action, prayers and reflection, we celebrate and respect the diversity of cultures and beliefs’ in the society, create a safe challenging learning environment, encourage each individual to reach his/her maximum potential. Our alluring curriculum and core values provide an opportunity for all to gain the skills and knowledge necessary to lead happy purposeful and successful life.

Our Vision is to reach the zenith of education and develop our institution into an educational hub which will enable the students to face the global challenges. The school aims at making its children well disciplined, good mannered, cooperative, obedient, dutiful, patriotic, punctual, secular, sincere, regular, respectful and responsible. The ultimate aim is to produce good human beings who can make a difference to the society and work to inculcate supreme qualities in the children who are the future “Architects of the Nation”.

Our Campus and Facilities

CCTV

The security and safety of the students is of prime importance and for this the school has a web of close circuit cameras installed in every nook and corner to keep an eye on any anomaly or deviation in children’s behavior. There are in all 52 cameras installed at various points to keep a close vigilance in every corner of the school and any activity in the school can be monitored anytime. The cameras are in working mode 24×7 and ensure that no suspicious element or activity hampers the children’s security.

GUARDS

The school has employed a number of male and female guards to ensure the safety of the children against any suspicious activity. The boys and girls studying in the school are under keen observation of 2 female and 1 male guards all the time.

FIRE EXTINGUISHERS

The school takes care to save the children from any kind of disaster. In case of a fire emergency the school children’s safety is a matter of utmost priority. There are 45 fire extinguishers installed in various blocks of the building. Moreover, Water Hydrant system is also available which is attached with water tanks of 90000 liters capacity in school campus to handle any fire disaster. The staff of the school is trained to handle safety equipments, initiate emergency evacuators and to protect the students in the event of a fire emergency.

MOD

The main duties and responsibilities of MOD include; protecting his/her students and facilitating the visitors if any. He/She also ensures that all students are in proper discipline and uniform. He/She supervises the arrangements at the time of morning assembly and smooth dispersal of students at off time. MOD ensures punctuality of all house teachers as well as students. Not only the Houses have their MODs, but the coordinators also deploy wing wise teachers on duty every day to keep an extra eye on special areas .The motive behind this surveillance is maintenance of proper discipline and safety of our students. It becomes the duty of the teacher to see that no class goes without a teacher and students do not move aimlessly without their passes in the school campus. Proper rules of the corridors are followed by the children of each wing and wishing teachers as well as outsiders, walking on the left of the corridors, maintaining silence in the corridor and if not in line, stepping out of the class in a squad.

School Gates

Schools are equally responsible for the safety of children. Our school has three gates for senior wing and one gate for Toddlers. These gates allow the teachers on duty and the hospitality staff who are deployed in the morning and at off time to verify visitors by seeking important information in their registers at the main gate and permit exit in emergency situations. With all the possible measures like CCTV, visitors’ passes and written information of the visitors, parents are guaranteed safety of their wards. Part time teachers also enter the school other than their duty hours only with permission.
